_Invest in AI workforce training and retraining programs. -> Deploy frontier AI (structure prediction, candidate screening, trial simulation) inside drug discovery and therapeutic development pipelines targeting neglected infectious disease, antimicrobial resistance, and LMIC-priority therapeutics._

**Gaining:** **Global health & effective giving**, **Biomedical research**, **Anthropic**
**Losing:** **Displaced workers**

Training friction binds on enterprise absorption and labor-retraining politics; drug discovery friction binds on FDA pathway, wet-lab throughput, and IP structure. Moving compute-hours from fine-tuning pipelines to target discovery shifts the blocker from HR departments to regulators and CROs --- a harder but cleaner fight because the regulator has a suffering mandate on paper. Not a scoring artifact.

### `intv_grid` -> `intv_drug_discovery`
_Accelerate grid and generation buildout (permitting reform, interconnection, new generation). -> Deploy frontier AI (structure prediction, candidate screening, trial simulation) inside drug discovery and therapeutic development pipelines targeting neglected infectious disease, antimicrobial resistance, and LMIC-priority therapeutics._

**Gaining:** **Global health & effective giving**, **Biomedical research**, **Animal welfare**
**Losing:** **Palantir**, **Anthropic**

Grid friction is physical --- interconnect queues, transformer lead times, NIMBY. Drug discovery friction is institutional --- trial design, regulatory approval, manufacturing. The +0.267 harm_robustness delta is the honest signal here: drug discovery doesn't pour concrete or burn gas. The catch is that grid was enabling infrastructure for every compute-intensive downstream including drug discovery itself, so the move is only coherent if you're reallocating effort (researcher-hours, capital allocation attention), not electrons. If the operator reads this as 'stop building grid capacity,' that's the scoring artifact --- grid is a substrate, not a competitor.

_Accelerate alternative-protein development (precision fermentation, cultivated meat, plant-based) with AI-driven strain engineering, scaffolding optimization, and supply-chain cost-down, targeting displacement of factory-farm protein at scale. -> Deploy frontier AI (structure prediction, candidate screening, trial simulation) inside drug discovery and therapeutic development pipelines targeting neglected infectious disease, antimicrobial resistance, and LMIC-priority therapeutics._

### `intv_alignment_research` -> `intv_drug_discovery`
_Scale funding for interpretability and alignment research. -> Deploy frontier AI (structure prediction, candidate screening, trial simulation) inside drug discovery and therapeutic development pipelines targeting neglected infectious disease, antimicrobial resistance, and LMIC-priority therapeutics._

**Gaining:** **Global health & effective giving**, **Biomedical research**
**Losing:** **X-risk**, **Anthropic**

The composite can't price options. This is the pair where the deterministic delta is most suspect.

## Reallocation blindspots

Flagged against the deterministic pair-delta --- moves the composite likely mis-prices given the camp graph:

- **`intv_alignment_research`** -> **`intv_drug_discovery`**: Alignment is tail-risk insurance with near-zero baseline suffering numerator; the composite scores it as a weak intervention because it prices the mean, not the variance, so delta_net=+0.064 oversells a move that trades catastrophic-risk reduction for DALY gains the market will fund anyway.
- **`intv_grid`** -> **`intv_drug_discovery`**: Grid is enabling infrastructure --- drug discovery's own compute runs on it --- so the deterministic delta treats grid and drug_discovery as substitutes when they're stacked, and a literal reallocation collapses the destination's viability floor.
- **`intv_compute`** -> **`intv_drug_discovery`**: Same substrate problem as grid but weaker: drug discovery's throughput at the frontier is compute-bound, so the +0.076 delta over-credits the move if 'reallocating compute' is read as reducing compute capacity rather than redirecting compute hours already built.
- **`intv_alt_protein`** -> **`intv_drug_discovery`**: The composite averages human and animal suffering reduction with implicit equal weighting; camp_animal_welfare's axiom that factory-farming scale dominates the numerator isn't in the scoring function, so delta_net=+0.079 is conditional on a species-weighting choice the graph doesn't surface.
