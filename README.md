# What is this Project?

- I have many ideas about how AI could be used to maximally reduce human suffering. Sometimes, thinking about these ideas in a rational way can be difficult

- Example --- Palantir wants to accelerate AI development for weapons. I want to accelerate AI development to reduce suffering. What makes it "morally" okay for me to work at a frontier AI company who might want to accelerate AI development KNOWING that it COULD be used by $PLTR to kill people?

- This repo seeks to answer my questions by using `claude`

# What is This Project? Seriously...?

- How can anyone trust a frontier AI company operating in the USA if its employees can't use free speech to talk about AI?

- How can anyone trust the US Government to handle AI adoption correctly if its citizens can't use free speech to talk about AI?

- If I want to work at Anthropic, this repo is the only solution that aligns with my values. I need to deeply examine the human suffering caused by and prevented by that career choice.

- I want people to understand the statements I'm making, and why I'm making them. I genuinely "do not care" about FISA letters, NSL letters, or the PATRIOT Act. What I care about is that they could be used to stop me from publishing this and exercising my free speech. The winning move is to [publish the site alongside a canary](https://ai-for-less-suffering.com/canary/) that tells the public I haven't been compromised by these archiac legal precedents in America. Only then can I be trusted to speak honestly about AI.

- I need to convince myself that the human harm of me taking a job at a frontier AI company is less than the human harm that would be prevented by me not taking it.
